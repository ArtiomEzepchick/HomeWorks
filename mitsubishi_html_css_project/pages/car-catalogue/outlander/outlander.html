Welcome the New 4th-generation OUTLANDER!

The fourth-generation Outlander debuted on February 16, 2021. It takes inspiration from the GT-PHEV concept which debuted at the 2016 Paris Motor Show, and the Engelberg Tourer concept which debuted at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show. Developed four years after Nissan purchased a 34 percent stake in Mitsubishi Motors, the vehicle is based on the Nissan Rogue/X-Trail (T33), riding on the CMF-CD platform, first ever for a Mitsubishi vehicle. It continues to offer the same seven-seat configuration as its predecessor, unlike the Nissan Rogue that only has two-row seating.

ABOUT US

Company Profile

Grodno Mitsubishi is a Mitsubishi dealership located in Grodno. Since our founding in 2008, Grodno Mitsubishi has been able to serve customers in a wide area, include Minsk, Vitebsk, Brest, Mogilev, Belastok and etc. We Wouldn't Be Here Without Our Customers!

Car shoppers visiting Grodno Mitsubishi will find an inventory stocked with all of the best models Mitsubishi has to offer. This includes models like the Eclipse Cross, ASX, Outlander, L200 and Pajero Sport. These vehicles blend value with performance and also offer a variety of practical features.

In addition to our new inventory, Grodno Mitsubishi offers an impressive selection of pre-owned cars, trucks and crossovers from Mitsubishi and multiple other brands. These pre-owned vehicles are carefully inspected before entering out inventory, ensuring we only offer the best used cars to our customers.

The Grodno Mitsubishi service center is here to keep your car in the best condition possible. We are able to handle everything from routine maintenance to more substantial repairs. While your car is in the shop, take advantage of our free Wi-Fi, specialty coffee, shuttle and kids' area.

Grodno Mitsubishi is an active member of our Belarus community. We support a variety of local champion boxers and MMA fighters. Also our dealer principal is friends with legendary president Alexander Lukashenko and his son Nikolay. We are also a significant supporter of hockey.

Take a look around the Grodno Mitsubishi Dealership website to learn more about the vehicle and services we have to offer. If you have any questions, our team is here to help.
